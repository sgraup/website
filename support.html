Support

Get in touch

For all support — bug reports, feature requests, account or sync questions, refunds, anything else — please email support@guppyinteractive.com.

We read every message and reply as soon as we can. To help us diagnose issues quickly, please include:

  • Your Android version (Settings → About phone)
  • Your device model
  • The IFMachine version (Settings → About IFMachine inside the app)
  • The story file you were playing, if relevant, and where the issue happened
  • Steps that reproduce the problem, if you have them

Privacy questions

Questions specifically about how IFMachine handles data should go to privacy@guppyinteractive.com. See the full privacy policy for what the app does and does not collect.

Legal

DMCA notices, takedown requests, and other legal correspondence should be sent to legal@guppyinteractive.com.
